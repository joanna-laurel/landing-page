Try changing the viewport width!

I made this flexible yet reasonably constrained text + button situation by expanding on what I learned from Josh Comeau's Interactive Guide to Flexbox. It includes negative bottom margins + compensatory padding to allow for a larger gap between the text and button when they are side-by-side without that carrying over when they are stacked vertically.
